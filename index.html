Nazrul Mancha

Nazrul Mancha is an auditorium in Kolkata, India. Named after Bengali poet Kazi Nazrul Islam, the auditorium is notably used as the venue for the Dover Lane Music Conference in January each year, where audiences enjoy musical performances for four consecutive nights.Ram Mohan Mission mostly celebrates its Annual Events here.

Rabindra Sarobar Stadium

The Rabindra Sarobar Stadium is a multi-use stadium in Lake Gardens, Kolkata, India. It is used for football matches and it serves as the home of many Calcutta Football League matches. The Annual sports day of Ram Mohan Mission is always celebrated here.

Founder Of Ram Mohan Mission And

Ram Mohan Mission High School

Rev. Shankar Biswas

(1938 - 2011)





Sri Sankar Biswas bathed in the visionary lights and blessings of three legendary souls Raja Rammohan Roy, Swami Vivekananda and Pt. Iswar Chandra Vidyasagar and held their ideals, thought mission, vision and deeds in his mind with highest respect and worked like an idealist, realist and positivist relentlessly throughout his life to eradicate fierce social evils & superstitions from the Indian society and brought about social crusade to reform Indian society in its root and branch.


He had a very compassionate vision with a mission to serve for the welfare of the masses with health services, education, environmental consciousness and raising awareness campaigns against the fierce social evils prevalent in the Indian society which are continuing till today.

It is Sri Sankar Biswas, as Founder President of the Mission, for the first time in India, undertook intensive nation-wide campaign for raising public awareness against social evils when he specially emphasized for elimination of female foeticide, a barbarous act of gender – discrimination. His nation-wide slogan was – “Save the girl child, Beti Nahi Bete Se Kom, Beti Bhachao, Desh Bachao.” He conducted nationwide campaigns for five times (2007-2010) with 100 volunteers, 8 buses & thousands of slogan leaflets & many banners & festoons. Sri Biswas with his team was welcomed & honored everywhere.



What's New

  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System


    1.Attendance Tracking In Real-Time

    A student attendance management system helps to track and store the attendance data in real-time. Besides student and staff attendance, the system also helps school admins in maintaining and tracking leaves applications, arrival & departure times, lunch breaks, and vacations of staff members & students.

    2.Automate The Payroll Process

    If the Payroll management software is coupled with the attendance management system, then the payroll process becomes automated and more efficient. The school HR can easily track absences and leaves of staff members at one glance, and adjust salaries as necessary based on that information. Using the system also reduces the chances of errors in the payroll system. Since the biometric system calculates the exact working hours, holidays, the HR team is less susceptible to make errors in tabulating the payroll.

    3.Absence of Proxy Attendance

    In many schools, especially the higher grades, students manipulate the daily attendance with proxies, buddy punching or tampering of records in a matter of concern. Using a biometric/RFID system with an attendance module makes it virtually impossible to fake attendance, as each individual’s biometric information is unique. This ensures that the attendance is accurate with no opportunity for student manipulation. It also improves the punctuality and the discipline of the students.

  • New School Canteens


    As part of the school environment, a canteen is in a unique position to make a positive contribution to students’ health and welfare. The functions of school canteens are to:

    1.provide a service to the school community

    2.provide a variety of nutritious and attractively presented food and drinks at a reasonable cost and reinforce classroom learning

    3.The canteen provides a rewarding opportunity for parents to be involved in their child’s school. A canteen can become an essential source of nutrition education that supports what students learn in the classroom and supports a whole school approach to healthy eating such as; the school produce garden.


    Advantages of School Canteens

    1.Convenience is one key advantage of canteens and cafeterias. Students do not need to leave the school for their lunches, which allows more time for eating and means that parents do not need to rush to pack a lunch in the morning.

    2.Improving a student's overall nutritional health is another benefit. Schools that participate in the National School Lunch program are required to provide a complete and balanced lunch for students .
